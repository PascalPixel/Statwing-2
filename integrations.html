Partner up with Statwing

Your users' data is only as valuable as their ability to analyze it.

If your web service creates or collects data, your users need a way to understand their data. You can build analysis tools. Or you can create data exports and hope they're skilled with Excel or traditional statistical tools.

Instead (or in addition), you can integrate with Statwing in under an hour, so all of your users can get as much value as possible out of their data.

Two forms of partnerships

Integrated

Embed Statwing into your product, with customized branding.

Your users will analyze their data with the full power of Statwing—and with your branding. Statwing can embed directly into your product, or in one click your users can analyze their data at https://[yourapp].statwing.com.

Contact about integration

Exporting (Free)

Your users can one-click export their data to Statwing.

Your users analyze their data for free initially. If they want to use Statwing for more than a month, they'll subscribe to Statwing.
