Integrations


Statwing is modern data analysis software, the power of statistical analysis, in an intuitive interface

If you have a web service that creates or collects data for your users, that data is only as valuable as your users' ability to analyze it.

You can build your own data analysis software. Or you can let users export data and hope they're skilled with Excel or traditional statistical tools. Instead (or in addition), you can integrate with Statwing, so all of your users can get as much value as possible out of their data with ease.

It takes less than an hour to integrate with Statwing. Once you've done so, your users will have one-click access to their data in Statwing.

Two forms of integration

Integrated

Your users will never know they've left your site: they'll see your logo, your colors, and your website name in the URL. Depending on your user interface, we might be able to embed into your product directly.

Users will get the full functionality of Statwing, complete with optional authentication through your sign-in system.

CTA: Contact Statwing About Integrating [which if clicked yields a form with fields for email address, website, and optional notes]

Exporting

At no cost to you, your users can one-click export their data to Statwing.

Users can analyze their data for free at first.

To save their work, export to powerpoint, or access other advanced features, they'll need to start a one-month trial of Statwing.

Instant Visualization

Statwing automatically creates beautiful, interactive visualizations along with every analysis.

In spreadsheets and statistical software, creating even simple charts takes too much time and effort.

Statwing understands your data's structure, so it automatically creates exportable histograms, scatterplots, heatmaps, and bar charts.
