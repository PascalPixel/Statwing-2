Modern Data Analysis Software

The power of statistical analysis software, in an intuitive interface.

Easier than Spreadsheets

Simply ask your data a question, and get a real answer.

Gender has a clear connection to Age

Asking a simple question of your data in a spreadsheet can be minutes of painstaking work shuffling data, creating pivot tables, charts, and writing formulas.

Unlike spreadsheets, Statwing is designed solely for analyzing tables of data, so its users say they work five to ten times faster than they do in spreadsheets.

So if you're analyzing a data and you're writing anything more than =AVERAGE(), you'll get your work done much faster in Statwing.

Statistical Analysis for Humans

Existing statistical software was built for statisticians, so they require expertise to ask even simple questions.

Statwing is built for business users and data analysts, so it chooses statistical tests automatically, even accounting for outliers or other issues in your data.

For experienced in statistical analysis users, you can dive into clean, interactive output of p-values, effect sizes, confidence intervals, and more.

Statwing also translates everything into plain, understandable English, so you can understand your data the same way a statistician or data scientist would.

Instant Visualization

Statwing automatically creates beautiful, interactive visualizations along with every analysis.

In spreadsheet and statistical software, even creating simple bar charts takes an inordinate amount decisions and clicks.

Statwing understands the structure of your data, though, so it instantly creates histograms, scatterplots, heatmaps, bar charts, anything your data calls for.

Those visualizations make data cleaning much faster, give a rich feel for the data, and can then be exported for use in presentation software.
